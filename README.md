Project Title:
Analyzing the Impact of Caffeine Consumption on Youth Productivity and Performance

Executive Summary:
This project applies Data Analysis and Business Intelligence techniques to study the impact of caffeine consumption on youth productivity, focus, and sleep quality. By analyzing survey-based health and lifestyle data, the project aims to identify optimal caffeine consumption levels, assess potential negative effects of excessive intake, and provide data-driven recommendations to improve productivity and well-being among young individuals

Project Objectives:

•	Analyze Caffeine Consumption Patterns among youth.
•	Measure Productivity Levels in relation to caffeine intake.
•	Study the Effect of Caffeine on Sleep Quality and Focus.
•	Identify Safe and Optimal Caffeine Consumption Ranges.
•	Develop Interactive Dashboards to support awareness and decision-making
